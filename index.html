About Rock Garden

Discover the Beauty of Rock Garden, Ranchi

Nestled on the scenic Gonda Hill and overlooking the serene Kanke Dam, Rock Garden Ranchi is one of the most iconic tourist attractions in Jharkhand. This breathtaking site is crafted from natural rock formations and features stunning sculptures, waterfalls, and a picturesque iron footbridge.

Inspired by the famous Nek Chand’s Rock Garden in Chandigarh, this unique destination blends artistic creativity with natural beauty. Visitors can explore intricately carved stone sculptures, enjoy a tranquil man-made waterfall, and walk across the charming suspension bridge that offers a mesmerizing view of Kanke Dam.

Whether you're a nature lover, photographer, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, Rock Garden is the best place to visit in Ranchi. The garden is also a popular picnic spot, offering visitors a chance to unwind in a stunning setting.
